Not all who wander are lost. Read Me!

Click on the markers in the map and add them to the list to help plan your trek through the Appalachian trail, it contains the cities, shelters, and points of interest you will encounter along your journey.

Shelters along the trail will always be free but you are not guaranteed a spot so always have a backup plan!

Be sure to stop along the small towns you'll run across for a hot shower or a night in a hotel room to take a break from the elements

Always be sure to minimize your impact on the nature you pass through, this means stay on the designated trails and practice "leave no trace" camping to help preserve our wonderful environment
